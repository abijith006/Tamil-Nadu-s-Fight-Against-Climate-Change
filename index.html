About Climate Action in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is at the forefront of climate action in India, with a strong commitment to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The state has implemented numerous initiatives to combat climate change, including renewable energy projects, afforestation drives, and climate resilience programs. These efforts aim to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, protect vulnerable communities, and promote sustainable development.

About This Website

This website is dedicated to raising awareness about climate action in Tamil Nadu. It provides information on the state's initiatives, supporting organizations, and related news. Our goal is to inspire individuals and organizations to take part in the fight against climate change and contribute to a sustainable future.
